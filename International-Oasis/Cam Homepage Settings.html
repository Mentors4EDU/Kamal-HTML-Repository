New full time staff member!

Alexa Lawlor will be raising funds to become a full time staff member for International Oasis. Alexa has worked as a volunteer for our organization since fall 2015, is TEFL certified, and has a passion for serving people from all over the world. Her role will be to be available to help OU international students, run our furniture bank, plan events, recruit host families, and build volunteer teams for our student services.
